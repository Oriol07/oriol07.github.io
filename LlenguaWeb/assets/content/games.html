Games

Alien Music Invaders

Aliens are descending on the city, each carrying a word in Catalan. The player must quickly type the English translation to destroy the alien ship before it lands. If the translation is correct, the alien ship is destroyed. Incorrect answers allow the alien to advance closer.

Adjectives

Adjective Match-Up is a fun matching game where players reinforce their knowledge of Catalan adjectives and their correct gender and number agreement with nouns. Players are shown a noun or an image, along with several adjective options. They must select the adjective that correctly agrees with the noun’s gender (masculine or feminine) and number (singular or plural).
